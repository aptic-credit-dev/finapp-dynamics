# Operational AI (m25)

## Purpose
AI assistance for Feedback and Case: sentiment analysis, complaint/feedback classification, summarization,
activity recommendation, and routing/escalation suggestions. 9 reference tables.

## Capabilities
Feedback + case summarization; sentiment + NLP classification; root-cause hints; suggested activities; workflow-
routing + escalation recommendations — all with confidence + citations and human review.

## Guardrails
Recommends only; never closes, escalates, or reassigns a controlled item on its own. Restricted data never leaves
approved providers. Every suggestion is audited.

## MVP
Feedback/case summaries + sentiment as human-reviewed suggestions.
