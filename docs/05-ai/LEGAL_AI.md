# Legal AI (m26)

## Purpose
AI assistance for the legal domain: document summarization + extraction, key-date extraction, and matter
briefing. 11 reference tables.

## Capabilities
Legal document summarization + extraction; court-date/deadline extraction; matter summaries; drafting assistance
— with confidence, citations, and human review, inside privilege + ethical-wall boundaries.

## Guardrails
Never files, never reaches a legal conclusion, never exposes privileged data to unauthorized users, never sends
restricted/privileged data to unapproved providers. Human legal review is mandatory.

## MVP
Deferred to fast-follow; read-only summaries where the legal portfolio is read-only.
