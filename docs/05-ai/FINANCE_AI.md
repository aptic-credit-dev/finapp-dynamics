# Finance AI (m27)

## Purpose
AI assistance for reconciliation and finance: transaction classification, match suggestions, anomaly detection,
and risk flagging. 12 reference tables.

## Capabilities
Reconciliation match suggestions (with confidence); transaction classification; anomaly detection; risk flagging;
journal-recommendation drafting assistance — all human-reviewed.

## Guardrails
**Never auto-posts.** Never approves. Suggestions feed draft journals + human approval only. Explainable matches;
restricted data stays with approved providers; every output audited.

## MVP
Reconciliation match suggestions as human-confirmable hints.
