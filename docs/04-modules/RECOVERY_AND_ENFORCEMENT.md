# Recovery & Enforcement (m17)

## Purpose
Manage debt recovery and security enforcement arising from legal matters: settlement, asset tracing, security
realization, and enforcement workflows. 38 reference tables.

## Capabilities
Recovery matter tracking; settlement; asset tracing; security realization; enforcement workflows; cost + outcome
tracking; links to legal matters and finance transactions; deadline reminders + escalation; audit.

## Shared services
Status/workflow/SLA/timeline (m06), documents (m09), audit, authz, outbox. Depends on Legal (m14/m16); feeds
finance transactions for reconciliation.

## MVP
Deferred (post-MVP). High-risk recovery automation is excluded from early releases and gated by the certification
process.
