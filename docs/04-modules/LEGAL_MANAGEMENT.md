# Legal Management (m14 + m16 litigation + m18 legal docs)

## Purpose
Manage litigation, demand letters, recovery matters, securities enforcement, contract/employment/regulatory
matters, with court tracking, advocate assignment, deadlines, risk classification, cost + outcome tracking, and a
legal document repository. Reference tables: m14 (23), m16 litigation (31), m18 legal docs (49).

## Capabilities
Legal matter creation + case-to-matter conversion; internal + external advocate assignment; court calendars,
mentions, hearings, rulings, judgments, appeals; filing deadlines with strong reminders + escalation; demand
letters; recovery matters; securities enforcement; legal risk classification; document repository + templates +
OCR + legal AI summaries; privilege + ethical walls; retention + legal hold.

## Privilege & ethical walls
Unauthorized users cannot infer privileged matter data (RLS FORCE + access rules). Ethical walls partition access
within a tenant.

## Shared services
Status/workflow/SLA/timeline (m06), documents (m09), audit, authz, outbox, legal AI (m26).

## MVP
Read-only portfolio: view matters, deadlines, documents. Write/convert/file paths are post-MVP.
