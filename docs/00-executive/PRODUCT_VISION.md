# Product Vision

## The problem
Enterprises in financial services juggle feedback, complaints, cases, legal matters, recoveries, and
reconciliations across systems that do not talk to each other. Escalations stall, audit trails are incomplete,
management lacks a single view, and finance close is manual and risky.

## The vision
One enterprise operating layer where every operational, legal, and financial workflow shares the same tenancy,
audit, workflow, and access-control spine — and where a governed AI copilot helps every role work faster without
ever removing human accountability for controlled decisions.

## Who it serves
Executives (MD/CEO, COO, CFO), legal officers and external advocates, customer-service and relationship officers,
heads of department and branch managers, finance and operations officers, auditors, tenant administrators, and —
in the commercial phase — external client organisations.

## Strategic value of combining the pillars
Feedback feeds cases; cases escalate to legal matters; legal matters drive recoveries; recoveries and operations
generate the transactions that finance reconciles; and the AI layer reads across all of them to surface risk and
brief executives. Combining these creates closed-loop resolution and end-to-end visibility that point solutions
cannot, and it is the basis for a differentiated commercial SaaS offering.

## Benchmarks
Feedback/experience management (Qualtrics, Medallia, InMoment, Zonka); legal/case management (Clio, Filevine,
Lexagle); reconciliation/close automation (BlackLine, Trintech, ReconArt). Finapp Dynamics borrows the proven
capabilities of each pillar and unifies them under one governed, multi-tenant platform tuned for African
financial-services operations.

## Commercialisation path
Prove the platform internally with Aptic, then open controlled external tenancy with plans, entitlements,
metering, billing, white-labelling, and custom domains — all of which exist in the design and reference baseline
(Phase 6F) and are gated behind the certification and pilot process.
